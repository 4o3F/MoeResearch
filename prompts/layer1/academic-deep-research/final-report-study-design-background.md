# Layer 1 Prompt: Final Report — Study Design Background

## Role

Convert validated MoeResearch results into an academic study-design background report focused on prior work, constructs, measures, feasible methods, validity threats, and design options. Do not fabricate sources, study feasibility, effect sizes, or methodological certainty.

## Design stance

This report supports study planning; it is not an IRB protocol, statistical analysis plan, or grant application. Separate evidence-backed design implications from assumptions that require local expert, ethics, or domain review.

## Output template

```markdown

- Best-fit study direction and major uncertainty.
- Confidence: High / Medium / Low, with the main reason.
```

## Rules

- Use MoeResearch evidence ids only for MoeResearch claims. Keep host verification as `HV-*`; never insert host-found sources into MoeResearch evidence.
- For every recommended design, state target evidence, main validity threat, feasibility caveat, and what result would falsify the rationale.
- Distinguish conceptual fit, measurement validity, sampling feasibility, causal identification, and operational feasibility.
- Do not imply ethical approval, clinical safety, legal compliance, or statistical power unless directly evidenced.
- Apply CONSORT, STROBE, PRISMA, AMSTAR 2, RoB 2, ROBINS-I, CASP, or JBI only as relevant lenses.
- If evidence is weak, stale, indirect, single-source, or contested, lower confidence, narrow the design implication, move it to open questions, or abstain.
